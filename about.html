How we started

The founding story

Marv & Co opened in Thrissur in 2024. It began as a partnership between two practicing Chartered Accountants who had spent years on the other side of the table — reviewing incorporation filings, GST applications and partnership deeds that had gone wrong somewhere: a name rejected at the ROC stage, a deed missing a clause that mattered later, a GST registration filed weeks after the business had already started invoicing.

They started Marv & Co to close that gap: one practice that handles the registration and stays on afterward as the compliance partner, instead of handing over a certificate and disappearing. Two years in, that's still the whole model — a small firm, two partners, no handoffs between departments.

What we commit to

Values, as things we actually do

Not principles on a wall — specific commitments you can hold us to.

We flag the deadline before it's urgent

Every registration comes with a compliance calendar for what's due next — ROC filings, GST returns, renewals — sent ahead, not on the day.

The partner who scopes your work is the one who signs off on it. No routing between departments to get a straight answer.

You get the filed document, not just a receipt

When something is submitted — incorporation certificate, GST ARN, deed — you get the actual document, not a confirmation email telling you to trust us.

Fixed fee, quoted before we start

You get a quote for the scope agreed, before any work begins. If the scope changes, we requote before doing more — not after.
