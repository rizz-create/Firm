Registration and the compliance that follows

Five services, each with a concrete outcome. Start the relevant checklist below, or book a consultation first if you're not sure which structure fits.

What you get

Services

Company Registration

Private limited or public company. You get a Certificate of Incorporation, company PAN and TAN, and the incorporation documents your bank will ask for — enough to open a current account and start invoicing as a registered company.

Start the registration checklist

LLP Registration

Limited liability protection with a partnership's flexibility. We draft the LLP agreement around your actual terms, secure DPIN/DIN for designated partners, and file with the ROC through to a registered, bank-ready LLP.

Start the registration checklist

Partnership Firm Registration

A partnership deed drafted around your real profit-sharing and decision-making terms — not a template — registered with the Registrar of Firms, with PAN applied for in the firm's name.

Start the deed questionnaire

GST Registration & Filing

From ARN to GSTIN, then ongoing monthly or quarterly return filing so returns go in on schedule — not the week before the deadline, and not after a notice arrives.

Start the GST registration form

Income Tax Return Filing

Your ITR prepared and filed for the previous year — we check your sources of income, GST status, directorships and shareholdings so nothing is missed, and file the correct return on time.
